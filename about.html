Hi there!

I'm Amandine, a Senior UX Designer at JobCloud, the leading digital company in the Swiss job market.

I’m currently UX project lead for a high-impact initiative (confidential for now) and prototyping AI-driven solutions, exploring how AI can make both job seeking and design workflows smarter. As co-lead of JobCloud’s design system, I align designers and developers to keep our products consistent, scalable, and user-friendly across platforms.

Before joining JobCloud, I was a UX designer at Levuro, a Swiss startup focused on developing an app for social media publishing and livestreaming. I also had the opportunity to work in Pittsburgh, USA, as a UI/UX designer for Marinus Analytics, a startup creating software to assist law enforcement in combating human trafficking. I also spent 4 years as a UX/UI designer at Bubble-Diving, an e-commerce site in France.

I have a bachelor’s degree in Web Design and another in Economics. I also have a special interest for UX writing and accessibility

I’m the proud owner of a little rescue dog named Anja, who has been by my side for six years. She’s an unofficial member of the team, attending stand-ups and even securing her own spot during design reviews.
