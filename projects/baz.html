Big Analytics Zurich website (BAZ)

BAZ needed a website to advertise their services and products.
They are an analytics team that offers support to other teams, and already made products.

1. A responsive website

Adobe XD was use to create high fidelity prototypes and define responsive breakpoints.
5 breakpoints were needed : 1330, 1025, 850, 560 and 360. At a width of 850, the top navigation is replaced by a hamburger menu. As the layout space is reduced, font size and images are sized down and the content is re organized. To avoid friction, some elements are hidden at small screen width to only keep the most important information.
