Wooly is an E-commerce mobile application.

The goal of the app is to offer a smooth online shopping experience from the choice of the wool to the checkout process.

Product page

Allows the customer to see the different colors of the product. Product details expand to reveal information.

Navigation drawer

Navigation drawers provide access to destinations in the app. It's controlled by a navigation menu icon.

Add to cart and checkout user flow

Allows the customer to save items for a later purchase. Checkout does not require an account creation, checkout as a guest is at the top of the page to avoid abandonment.
